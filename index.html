🎯 Key Features Across CX-Series

  • 7 complete coagulation parameters (PT, APPT, Fibrinogen, Thrombin Time, D-Dimer, FDP, AT-III)
  • 400-450 tests/hour high-throughput capacity
  • Advanced optical detection with multiple wavelengths
  • 1st & 2nd Derivative kinetic measurement (CX-9000)
  • 800nm near-infrared capability for hemolytic samples (CX-9000 exclusive)
  • Automated 7-step APPT mixing study with 120-minute incubation (CX-9000)
  • Turbidimetric/kinetic detection for accurate clot formation measurement
  • Temperature-controlled sample processing (37°C ± 0.2°C)
