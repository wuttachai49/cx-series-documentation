🎯 Key Features Across CX-Series

  • 7 complete coagulation parameters (PT, APPT, Fibrinogen, Thrombin Time, D-Dimer, FDP, AT-III)
  • 400-450 tests/hour high-throughput capacity
  • Advanced optical detection with multiple wavelengths
  • 1st & 2nd Derivative kinetic measurement (CX-9000)
  • 800nm near-infrared capability for hemolytic samples (CX-9000 exclusive)
  • Automated 7-step APPT mixing study with 120-minute incubation (CX-9000)
  • Turbidimetric/kinetic detection for accurate clot formation measurement
  • Temperature-controlled sample processing (37°C ± 0.2°C)

🔴 Why CX-9000's 120-Minute Mixing Study Matters

The Problem: Developing Factor VIII inhibitors require 30-120 minutes to fully activate at body temperature. Competitors with 12-15 minute incubation MISS these. A patient appears to have simple factor deficiency, doctor gives Factor VIII → it doesn't work because the developing inhibitor inactivates it.

The CX-9000 Solution: 120-minute automated incubation catches these developing inhibitors. Doctor knows to use bypass therapy (FEIBA/Novoseven) → works. Patient gets correct treatment → patient safety advantage.

Clinical Impact: This is not just a feature difference - it's a patient safety difference. CX-9000 saves patient lives through more accurate diagnosis.
